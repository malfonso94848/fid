What is "Splat Fest?"

Splat Fest is a free, semi-annual event that takes place all over the globe. Every six months, you are invited to take place in the largest paintball event in the entire world where you have a chance to earn over 100,000 dollars for your victory!

Why is "Splat Fest" a thing?

Splat Fest is an event paid for and sponsored by several of the worlds largest painball manufacturers in attempts to show the world how awesome paintball is to this day!

How can I participate?

There are many ways to support and participate in Splat Fest. Simply attending each event will continue to fund further Splat Fest's for years to come!
