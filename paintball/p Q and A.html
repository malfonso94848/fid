Will I need to bring by own gear?

While any participant is allowed to bring their own gear to Splat Fest and any contests involved, it it not a requirement. Free gear will be avaiable to those that do not own their own equipment but such borrowed gear MUST be returned at the end of their use.

Is ammunition free?

Free ammo will be provided for your use at participating locations. The ammount provided may change from area to area but you are guarenteed at least 50-100 free shots! Custom ammunition and other ammounition besides default paintballs are not provided.

Can anyone join?

You must be over the age of 18 to participate in the event, 16 with parent permission. All individuals who do sign up are required to sign a liability waiver.

Are there any gear restrictions?

Within Splat Fest, fully automatic paintball guns are NOT allowed for use. Your gear must be semi-automatic and cannot fire paintballs with more force than the average paintball gun. Failure to comply with these rules will have your gear temporarily revoked or may result in a bad from Splat Fest.
